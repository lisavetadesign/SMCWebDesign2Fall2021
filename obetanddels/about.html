ABOUT
obet & del's
Heather and Josh's roots run deep in the area and, in their words, wanted to give back to their community by creating a business driven by purpose.
For Heather and Josh, that means running Obet and Del's as a neighborly space where local residents can gather while enjoying tasty coffee and pastries. While the pandemic has created unpredictability and many challenges, Obet and Del's is thankful for their neighbors’ support and look forward to furthering its mission to build community, one coffee at a time.
We’re a family business; woman-owned, black-owned, filipino-owned and loving every bit of the culture we’re cultivating..
Josh
