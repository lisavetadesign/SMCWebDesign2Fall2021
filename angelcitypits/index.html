Welcome to angel city pit pulls

Our organization started as a group of like-minded pit bull fans doing independent rescue work and has grown into a collaborative and supportive village.
We are dedicated to being a resource for everything pit bull in Los Angeles, the "City of Angels". Please join us as we Educate humans about pit bulls, Advocate on behalf of pit bulls and Celebrate these amazing dogs.
